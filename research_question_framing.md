# Research Question Framing

## Selected Lane
Search & Discovery

## Research Question
How can we improve search results so that users can find the most relevant products or information more easily?

## Unit of Analysis
The unit of analysis is a user's search query and the search results shown for that query.

## Expected Output
The expected output is a ranked list of search results where the most relevant results appear first.

## Decision
The system decides which search results should be shown at the top based on how relevant they are to the user's query.

## Action
Users can click on the most relevant result, find what they are looking for faster, and complete their task more easily.

## Cost of a Wrong Recommendation
If the search results are not relevant, users may have trouble finding what they need. This can make them frustrated, reduce their trust in the platform, and they may leave without completing their task.

## Why Machine Learning Can Help
Machine learning can learn from previous search data and user interactions. Instead of relying only on fixed rules, it can improve the ranking of search results over time and provide more relevant results for different types of queries.

## Cautious Language
This project will explore whether machine learning can improve search result ranking. The final conclusion will depend on the results of the experiments and evaluation.
